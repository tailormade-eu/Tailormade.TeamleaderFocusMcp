# Business Types

Each country has a specific set of business types or range of legal entities. You can specify the business type when adding or updating a company.

## [📄️ businessTypes.listGet the names of business types (legal structures) a company can have within a certain country, sorted alphabetically.](/docs/api/business-types-list)
